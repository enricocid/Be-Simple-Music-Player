<i>Be Simple Music Player</i> is an extremely minimal local Music Player based on <a href="https://f-droid.org/packages/com.iven.musicplayergo/">Music Player GO</a>.

<b>Features:</b>

- Minimalistic interface
- Equalizer
- Light, dark, automatic themes
- Simple audio focus and headsets management
- Search, sorting ...
